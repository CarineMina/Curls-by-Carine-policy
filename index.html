✨ Booking & Salon Policy

1. Deposit Policy

A deposit is required to confirm your appointment. Appointments without a deposit are not considered confirmed.

2. Cancellation & Rescheduling

Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance for any cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows will result in losing the deposit.

3. Late Policy

Please arrive on time. A grace period of 20 minutes is allowed. If you arrive later than 20 minutes, your session may be canceled, and the deposit will not be refunded.

4. Hair Condition & Pricing

Prices may vary depending on hair density and condition. Extra charges may apply for very thick or highly textured hair.

5. Guests Policy

You may bring only one guest.

6. Preparation Policy

Clients must follow required hair preparation instructions before the appointment. Failure to do so may result in session cancellation or additional fees. In case of cancellation, the deposit is non-refundable.

7. Right to Refuse Service

We reserve the right to refuse service in cases of inappropriate behavior or unrealistic expectations.
