✨ Booking & Salon Policy

A deposit is required to confirm your appointment. Appointments without a deposit are not considered confirmed.

2. Cancellation & Rescheduling

Notify us 24h in advance. Late cancellations or no-shows will result in losing the deposit.

3. Late Policy

Grace period: 20 mins. Arriving later may result in cancellation and loss of deposit.

4. Hair Condition & Pricing

Prices vary by density/condition. Extra charges apply for very thick or highly textured hair.

5. Guests Policy

You may bring only one guest.

6. Preparation Policy

Clients must follow prep instructions. Failure to do so may result in extra fees or cancellation (Deposit non-refundable).

7. Right to Refuse Service

We reserve the right to refuse service for inappropriate behavior or unrealistic expectations.
